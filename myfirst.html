Fun Facts about Ooty (Unordered List)
  • Cows Wear Sweaters in Winter! In many local villages around Ooty, especially in colder months, people knit sweaters for their cows to keep them warm.
  • Alcohol-Free Zones Exist! Some parts of Ooty, especially near religious or eco-sensitive areas, are declared dry zones — meaning alcohol sales are restricted or banned.
  • Flowers Are Trained to Grow in Shapes! During the Ooty Flower Show, floral sculptures shaped like animals or trains are made by training live plants.
